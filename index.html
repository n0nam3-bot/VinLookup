How it works

1) Enter a 17-character VIN and click Decode — the free NHTSA vPIC API returns year, make, model, engine, fuel type, and more.
2) Decoded specs are shown in a clean table. Key fields like displacement, cylinders, and fuel type are used to match your vehicle to the parts database.
3) Parts output shows all compatible replacement parts with real brand names and OEM part numbers, grouped by category (filters, belts, brakes, cooling, electrical, body, and more).
4) Optionally load a local CSV/JSON parts catalog — matched rows appear above the built-in parts data.
